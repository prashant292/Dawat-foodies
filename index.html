About Us

Our Story

The Dawat Restaurant - The DR - reinvents the neighborhood eatery, combining timeless inspiration from classic Indian with inventive and playful modern flair. With influences ranging dinner to the elegant railway dining cars of days past, you'll find a nostalgic collection of comforts, classics and curiosities with a modern twist.We provide every type of food like Indian, American, Chinese, Italian etc.Located in the heart of the people, the Dawat Restaurant is inspired by the exciting and traditional flavors of Indian Species.
